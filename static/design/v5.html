↑ Voronoi Glass — at rest the image appears perfectly assembled. Move your mouse anywhere on the page, touch, or tilt your device to shatter the image into glass-shard refractions. Each cell displaces the image beneath it uniquely. Transitions use V4's crystallization grow. The tessellation is invisible — you only see it through the refraction differences.
