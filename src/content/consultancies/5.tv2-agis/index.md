As part of a complete rebrand of TV 2 Denmark their marketing department, had to transition it's flow branding out of a legacy system.

They tasked us to build an in-house system for consolidating their corporate on-air branding, broadening the scope from flow trailers to branding of all motion campaign elements in the house including versioning for SoMe formats.

We decided to write a new hybrid kubernetes/on-prem solution from zero using SvelteKit and the excellent open source project nexrender.

The system is called AGIS, an acronym for Automatisk Grafik Indpaknings System (Automatic Graphics Wrapping System).
